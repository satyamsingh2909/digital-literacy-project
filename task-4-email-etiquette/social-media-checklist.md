# 📱 Social Media Responsibility Guide for Students

## Do’s

1. **Maintain a Professional Tone:** Always use respectful and polite language in your posts and comments. This helps in building a positive and professional digital image.
2. **Keep Your Profile Clean and Appropriate:** Ensure that your profile picture, bio, and shared content reflect a responsible and professional personality suitable for academic environments.
3. **Verify Information Before Sharing:** Always check the authenticity of news, messages, or content before posting to prevent the spread of misinformation.
4. **Use Strong and Secure Passwords:** Protect your accounts by using strong, unique passwords and updating them regularly to avoid unauthorized access.
5. **Think Before You Post:** Consider the long-term impact of your content on your reputation, as online posts can remain permanent.

## Don’ts

1. **Avoid Sharing Personal Information:** Do not share sensitive details such as your phone number, address, passwords, or OTPs on public platforms.
2. **Do Not Post Inappropriate Content:** Avoid sharing content that is offensive, abusive, or unprofessional, as it can harm your image.
3. **Stay Away from Online Arguments:** Do not engage in unnecessary debates or conflicts, as they can negatively affect your online reputation.
4. **Avoid Clicking Suspicious Links:** Do not click on unknown or suspicious links, as they may lead to phishing attacks or malware.
5. **Do Not Overshare:** Avoid sharing excessive personal details about your daily life or private matters, as it can compromise your privacy.
