# 🔐 Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Do not click on unknown links or messages from unverified sources.
- Always verify the sender before sharing any personal information.
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Avoid downloading apps or files from unofficial websites.
- Keep your phone and apps updated to the latest version.

## Financial / UPI Safety
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money — it is only required to send payments.
- Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people claiming to transfer money.
- Always double-check payment requests before approving them.
- Never share OTP, bank details, or UPI PIN with anyone.

## Awareness & Responsible Usage
- Be cautious of urgent messages creating pressure to act quickly.
- Cross-check offers or payment requests that seem too good to be true.

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report cyber fraud immediately at: [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
- Helpline Number: 1930 (24×7 support for financial fraud cases)
